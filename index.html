Dalil Al-Enzar

Dalil Alarm for Safety Tools and Materials is a pioneer in providing inspection, testing and maintenance of all fire protection activities and systems. We have been providing our services to commercial, industrial, governmental and commercial facilities since 1994. With the passage of time, we have expanded our services by adding qualified technical personnel to work in the fields of safety and fire systems. We also keep pace with the development of firefighting equipment in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and we have added access control systems, sales and maintenance of fire extinguishers and fire protection systems management software to our quality solutions catalog.
